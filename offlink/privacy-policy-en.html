OffLink Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

H Labs Works ("we", "us", or "our") operates the mobile application "OffLink" ("the App"). This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. About the App

OffLink is a group voice calling application that uses Wi-Fi hotspot and WebRTC technology without requiring an internet connection. Voice data is transmitted locally between devices and is never sent to external servers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Directly by the App

InformationPurposeStorage
Username (optional)Member identification during callsOn-device only (SharedPreferences)
Group information (group name, SSID, password)Group call configuration and connectionOn-device only (SharedPreferences)
Point balance and historyIn-app point system managementOn-device only (encrypted)
Device information (OS version, battery level, memory)Host selection scoringDuring call session only (not persisted)

About voice data: Call audio is encrypted using WebRTC (DTLS-SRTP) and is transmitted only between devices on the local network. Voice data is never sent to our servers or any external servers.

2.2 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may independently collect information.

Google AdMob (Advertising)

  • For ad delivery, Google may collect advertising IDs (Android: AAID), device information, approximate location, and app usage data.
  • Collected data may be used to deliver personalized advertisements.
  • Details: Google Privacy Policy

Firebase (Planned for future release)

  • We plan to integrate Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore for anonymous authentication and app usage data collection.
  • This policy will be updated upon integration.

3. Location Information

The App uses Android's location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) to control Wi-Fi hotspot functionality and retrieve SSID information. This permission is required by the Android OS for Wi-Fi-related APIs and is not used to track, record, or transmit your geographic location.

4. Camera

The App uses camera permission for QR code scanning. Images captured by the camera are used solely for QR code analysis and are not stored or transmitted.

5. Microphone

The App uses microphone permission for group voice calls. Recorded audio is transmitted in real-time only to members on the same local network and is never recorded or stored on any device or server.

6. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Information described in Section 2.2 is automatically transmitted to Google AdMob for ad delivery.
  • When required by law.
  • With your consent.

7. Advertising Opt-Out

If you prefer not to receive personalized ads, you can change your settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

8. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All data stored directly by the App (username, group information, points) is saved on your device only.
  • Uninstalling the App will delete all local data.
  • For data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's privacy policy for deletion options.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or on this page.
